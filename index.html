Porto de Galinhas is a complete destination that appeals to different types of travelers, from those looking for tranquility and contact with nature to those looking for fun and more exciting activities. Below are some popular reasons to choose this destination


Paradisiacal beaches: Porto de Galinhas is famous for its white sand beaches and crystal clear water, ideal for bathing, diving and water sports

natural pools: the natural pools formed by coral reefs are one of the highlights of the region, they are incredible places to snorkel and observe life
